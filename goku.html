Goku

Profile

Goku is one of the main heroes of Dragon Ball. He is known for his love of training, his optimism, and his willingness to protect others even at great personal cost.

Over the series, Goku continues to improve through hard work and strong relationships. His story highlights themes of growth, perseverance, and fighting for what is right.

Why fans like him

Goku’s character is inspiring because he never gives up. He treats opponents with respect, learns from failures, and pushes himself to improve. Even when the odds seem impossible, he stays calm and focuses on the next step.
