Vegeta

Profile

Vegeta is the proud Prince of all Saiyans. He is known for his strong will, fierce determination, and competitive personality.

At first, Vegeta appears cold and arrogant, but over time he grows into a more complex character who cares deeply about his family, his pride, and his role as a protector of Earth.

Why Vegeta is important

Vegeta is one of the most interesting characters in Dragon Ball because he changes a lot throughout the story. He starts as an enemy, but later becomes one of the heroes. His journey shows growth, discipline, and the importance of never giving up.
